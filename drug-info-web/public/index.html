First Aid Guide

OFFLINE MODE • QUICK REFERENCE

1 CPR (Cardiac Arrest)

Push Hard & Fast: Center of the chest.

Rate: 100-120 compressions per minute (Match the beat of "Stayin' Alive").

Depth: At least 2 inches (5 cm) deep.

2 Severe Burns

Run COOL water for 10-20 mins.
Do NOT use Ice or Butter.
Cover loosely with a clean, dry cloth or bandage.

3 Choking (Heimlich)

Stand behind the person. Wrap arms around waist.

Make a fist above their navel (belly button).

Thrust INWARD and UPWARD quickly.
