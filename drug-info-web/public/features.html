Platform Features

Detailed breakdown of MediBot AI capabilities.

Gemini 2.5 Vision AI

Our core engine uses Google's latest Multimodal AI. It can read text from medicine labels, identify pills by shape/color, and analyze complex medical leaflets in seconds.

Interaction Safety Engine

Prevents dangerous drug combinations. By cross-referencing FDA databases, MediBot alerts you if two medications (e.g., Aspirin and Warfarin) shouldn't be taken together.

OpenFDA Integration

Real-time connection to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration. We pull the absolute latest data on side effects, recalls, and black-box warnings.
